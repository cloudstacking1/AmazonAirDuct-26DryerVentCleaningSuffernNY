FAQs

How often should I get my air ducts cleaned?
Typically every 3-5 years, or sooner if you’ve done renovations or have indoor pets.
Can dirty vents really cause allergies?
Yes. Dust, pollen, and mold spores in your ducts circulate into the air you breathe daily.
How long does a duct cleaning service take?
Most residential cleanings take between 1.5 to 3 hours depending on the home size.
